Automapper V0.1

Match your data against existing UK ward or local authority boundaries and create an easy-to-read shaded map.

1. Copy and paste your data

Two ways to use Automapper

Datasets with names
Create a dataset with TWO columns. (Download the template - .csv, .XLSX, Google Sheets)
Column one should contain the ward or the Local Authority name.
Column two should contain the attribute (numbers only) that you want to map.

Copy and paste the dataset into the Automapper.

List of Postcodes
Copy and paste a list of postcodes into the Automapper

Your data is safe.

Even though GeoMapper is a web app, the data you insert will be processed only by the web browser. No server-side operations or storages are performed, no one will see, touch or copy your data!

2. Explore your data

View how your specially generated map. Understand how your data varies across UK Local Authority or Ward Boundaries. Spot patterns in your data by comparing colors.

3. Download you Maps

Download your map or aggregated dataset. To compare the distribution of two attributes, take screenshots and place them side by side next to each other.
